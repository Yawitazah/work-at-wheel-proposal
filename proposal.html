Execution-Heavy Work Is Scoped Separately

  • Full website builds, funnel builds, major branding projects, and redesigns.
  • Ad campaign management, ad spend, paid campaign execution, and campaign-generated revenue.
  • Heavy video editing, full podcast episode production, and advanced content production.
  • Event execution labor, daily social posting, unlimited revisions, or unlimited meetings.
  • Contractor costs, software costs, printing, media buying, and custom member client work.

Boundary note: Zah Brand Solutions is being retained to build, direct, and manage the growth system, not to provide unlimited production labor inside the retainer. Production-heavy deliverables, campaign execution, ad spend, contractor costs, and custom client fulfillment should be quoted, approved, and scheduled separately.
